Mark Dahl lives and works in the same place where he was raised: at the foot of the majestic Ruby Mountains in rural, northeastern Nevada. He grew up as a cowboy, working on his father's ranch, as well as others in the area. In 1982, he founded Mark Mfg. and began life as a silversmith. He started making custom, silver-mounted bits and spurs. As a self-taught artist, Mark worked hard to master his craft.
